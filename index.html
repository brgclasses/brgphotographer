My Story

I like to capture the ecstatic moments and have photograhped wedding, street, events, nature and fashion throughout Nepal and India.

I still remember the day, 11th July, when I purchased my first DSLR camera, Canon EOS 220D. Before, that I used to capture through my phone camera. But I always felt I couldn't able to give proper justice to the true essence of the beautiful clicks.

And, then enters Canon EOS 220D in my life and it completely changed my perspective on beauty.

Although, initially it took me a while to get used to this amazing device. But, as it says, "Practice makes a man perfect", I gain good command over the handling of the DSLR.

Since, then, it is me and my DSLR whose friendship captured many beautiful moments ranging from natural beauties to beautiful females, street moments to fashion etc etc.
